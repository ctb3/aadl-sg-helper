How it works

📷 Photograph a sign. Get close — fill the frame with the sign, and keep house numbers or bin labels out of the shot.

🔍 The app reads the handwriting. Most codes are read instantly; tricky ones get a closer look from Claude.

🏆 Connect your AADL account and codes are submitted automatically for every player you check — or skip that, and you'll get a prefilled aadl.org link to submit each code yourself.

✍️ If a read is wrong or AADL rejects it, you can always fix the code by hand.

About

Point your camera at an AADL Summer Game lawn sign and this app reads the handwritten code and submits it to your Summer Game account(s) — or hands you a prefilled aadl.org link if you'd rather submit it yourself.

A personal project, not affiliated with the Ann Arbor District Library. (More to come here.)
